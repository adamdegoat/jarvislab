◇ How it works
Each cycle it dreams up new strategy ideas, then puts each through a brutal validation gauntlet — out-of-sample testing, statistical significance, parameter robustness, and an alpha gate that demands the idea beat simply holding the market (so bull-market luck fails). Survivors face an adversarial red-team that tries to kill them. The few that pass are incubated (paper-traded forward with no money) and only promoted to the book if they keep working. Most ideas die — and that is the point. The graveyard below is the brain showing its work.
