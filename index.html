How it works
Every idea has to survive this pipeline. Almost none do — and that's the whole point.
1
Invent
Proposes a brand-new strategy idea — and must give a real reason it should work, not just "the numbers fit."
2
Backtest
Replays the idea trade-by-trade across years of real historical prices.
3
The gauntlet
8 strict tests: it must be genuinely profitable, statistically real, work on data it never saw, survive trading costs, hold up when its settings are nudged — and crucially, beat simply buying and holding the market (so a lucky bull run isn't mistaken for skill).
4
Red-team
AI critics actively try to destroy each survivor — is it overfit? is the logic real? will it stop working? It's rejected unless it survives them.
5
Incubate
The rare survivor is paper-traded forward with zero money, to prove it still works on brand-new days it couldn't have been tuned on.
6
Promote or retire
If it keeps working, it earns a slice of the paper book. If it fades, it's retired to the graveyard.
